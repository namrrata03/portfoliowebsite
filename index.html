Recent Works

My projects done during college days.

Personal Branding

In my personal branding project, I crafted an identity that truly reflects who I am. By incorporating a side face of a woman into the letter 'n' of my name, I convey my feminine strength and individuality. The choice of dark grey, pink, and blue hues mirrors my personality unapologetically feminine, confidently bold, and effortlessly chic.

Publication Design

In my publication design project, I crafted a visually captivating book guide for styling. I focused on creating layouts that draw the reader in with their uniqueness, ensuring each page had a clear hierarchy of information. By combining interesting layouts, clear hierarchy, and clear imagery, I aimed to create a book that inspires the reader to know about styling with enthusiasm.

UI UX Case Study

In my UI/UX project, I redesigned the CLAIMS portal on our college HR website. This portal is crucial for faculty members to streamline their reimbursement process. The existing system is a hassle—it's slow, complicated, and tracking claims is like searching for a needle in a haystack. My redesign aims to make submitting, tracking, and managing claims easier.
